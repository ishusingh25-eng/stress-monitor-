About Us

The Stress Monitoring System is an innovative platform designed to monitor and assess stress levels using electroencephalography (EEG). Our system delivers accurate, real-time insights, enabling individuals, researchers, and organizations to better understand and manage stress actively.

Our Mission

Our team is dedicated to leveraging EEG technology to create a reliable, accessible, and effective stress monitoring solution that empowers people to take control of their mental well-being.

Our Team Aim

  • Provide real-time stress detection and analysis with cutting-edge technology.
  • Support individuals in identifying stress patterns and managing their mental health effectively.
  • Empower organizations to improve workplace well-being and productivity through data-driven insights.
  • Contribute to research and innovation in neuroscience and mental health technologies.
